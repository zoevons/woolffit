Zumba®

ZUMBA® is a fusion of Latin and International music - dance themes that create a dynamic, exciting, effective fitness system! The routines feature aerobic/fitness interval training with a combination of fast and slow rhythms that tone and sculpt the body. Zumba utilizes the principles of fitness interval training and resistance training to maximize caloric output, fat burning and total body toning. It is a mixture of body sculpting movements with easy to follow dance steps.

What should I wear to a Zumba® class?

Comfortable clothing such as tracksuit bottoms, t-shirt or vest top and a good sports bra. You'll also want to wear trainers. Dance aerobic trainers like NIKE Air Divine or NIKE Musique or NIKE Air Kapella. This sole has a light grip or ‘spin spot’ which will help you twist or turn. Cross trainers or running shoes are not ideal as they are made to propel the body forwards and backwards and have a heavy grip.

What should I bring to class?

Bring bottled water and a towel if needed, burning 300-1000 calories can be pretty sweaty and the most important thing is a sense of humour.

Is it important that I know how to dance?

No, Zumba® is designed for everyone, no dance or fitness experience necessary. People of many different ages, body types, backgrounds, dance and fitness levels all come together to have fun and get into fabulous shape with Zumba®.

I've never done ZUMBA® before?

It doesn’t matter it is all about having FUN. The most important thing to remember when you are in a class, allow yourself to enjoy the class, it doesn't matter if you get the moves wrong as long as your moving.
