Woolfmeister Online Studio Classes

Are you a Mum, a busy person, a working person, retired or even a person who has done classes with me before, whether it's Zumba, Kettlebell, Spin, HIIT, Body Conditioning, Fitness Pilates etc and miss my fun way of working out? Then these classes are for you so come and join me on a journey to fitness and well being.

What classes are included?

Every day classes will be added to a closed VIP group for you to do/try. These will include Spin Fit, Lift & Lean, Kettlebell, Fitness Pilates, HIIT, Thighs Not Pies, Non Equipment Workouts, Suns Out Guns Out, Mid Section Blast, Low Impact Workouts and many more!

Where will I find the classes?

You will get a link to my NEW YouTube channel each month. So if you have a favourite or the one's posted that day are not for you, you can do my others on the YouTube channel. There will be a timetable of the classes that are happening so you can plan your workouts. All of the classes are 20 mins or less, so they are easy to fit in around your schedules. Feel free to repeat them, or even add few together!

What else is included?

We will have fun challenges happening like the ones we trialed in the summer - Bring Sally Up etc to help motivate you. Also in the closed Facebook group you can ask me questions and I will also be sharing tips, information and technique videos. There will be Facebook LIVES so you get to know me and YOU GET TO WORKOUT WITH THE WOOLFMEISTER! There really is something for everyone.

Still not sure? Come and have a look at my Facebook page and try out some of the free classes!

This sounds great! How much does it cost?

You pay every 4 weeks the sum of £20.00 via PayPal, direct debit or bank transfer. You will then be added to the closed VIP Facebook Group and you are ready to go! Towards the end of the 4 weeks you are reminded to pay for the next month, if you don’t pay, or it’s not for you, you are taken out of the closed VIP Facebook Page. There are NO tie-ins and there is NO joining fee. So what are you waiting for - come and workout with the Woolfmeister!!
